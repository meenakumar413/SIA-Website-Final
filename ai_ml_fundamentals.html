AI & Machine Learning Fundamentals

Discover the foundational concepts of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) — from pattern recognition to real-world applications shaping the future.

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence enables machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence — such as reasoning, learning, and decision-making.

What is Machine Learning?

Machine Learning is a branch of AI where computers learn from data, identify patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention.

Real-World Applications

AI and ML power technologies like voice assistants, recommendation systems, self-driving cars, and personalized learning tools.

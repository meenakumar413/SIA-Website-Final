AI Agents Fundamentals

Learn how AI agents think, learn, and act — the building blocks that power today’s intelligent systems and digital assistants.

What Are AI Agents?

AI agents are programs that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve goals autonomously.

Types of AI Agents

Reactive agents respond to current situations; model-based agents plan using internal knowledge; goal-based and learning agents adapt through feedback and experience.

How Agents Work

Agents use sensors to gather information, apply logic or learned patterns, and use actuators to interact with their surroundings.

Applications

AI agents are behind chatbots, recommendation systems, robotics, and automated decision-making tools used in everyday life.
